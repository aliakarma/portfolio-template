Place the following files in this folder:

1. profile.jpg      — Your profile photo (square, at least 400×400 px)
2. YOUR_NAME_CV.pdf — Your CV/resume as a PDF
3. favicon.ico      — Your site favicon (optional — a default is used if missing)

Then update siteConfig.js:
  cv: "/YOUR_NAME_CV.pdf"   ← match the filename you used
